Imaging Dat Commons

The Imaging Data Commons (IDC) is a cloud-based data commons within the NCI Cancer Research Data Commons (CRDC) that connects researchers with cancer imaging datasets and resources for exploring those datasets and identifying relevant cohorts for computation.
